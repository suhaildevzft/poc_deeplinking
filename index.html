If the app is not opening on iOS:

  1. Ensure the Flutter app is installed on your iOS device
  2. Check that the app has the correct URL scheme: deeplinkpoc
  3. Try manually opening: deeplinkpoc://example.com/dashboard in Safari
  4. Test from Safari browser (not Chrome or other browsers)
  5. Make sure iOS version supports the deep linking method
